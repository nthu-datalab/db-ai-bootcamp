DB/AI Bootcamp

Consume the DBMS kernel knowledge and concatenate it with AI

Description

DB/AI bootcamp is for those students who have programming skills, but do not explicity understand how DBMS works.

In this bootcamp, we will lead everyone to understand how DBMS work and its details and let students try to modify the open source system. So, the goal of this camp is that students can implement their idea in DBMS quickly. In addition, we also combine some AI issue in this camp so that students can understand the latest AI knowledge and think outside the box.

Prerequisites

This course is intended for students who understand

  • OOP Programming,
  • Multi-threaded Programming,
  • Data Structure, and
  • Version Control.
We use Java as the main programming language throughout the course. Although not required, background knowledge about operating system will be helpful.
